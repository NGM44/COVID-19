What is COVID-19?

COVID-19 is the infectious disease caused by the most recently discovered coronavirus. This new virus and disease were unknown before the outbreak began in Wuhan, China, in December 2019. COVID-19 is a disease caused by the “novel corona virus”. Common symptoms are Fever, Dry Cough and Breathing difficulty. Some patients also have aches and pains, nasal congestion, runny nose, sore throat or diarrhoea
